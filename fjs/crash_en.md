# A Beginner's Crash Course in the FJS

So, you are intrigued by what the FJS has to offer, and would like to learn to use it.

This "crash course" was designed specifically with a focus on fast acquisition of all the fundamental elements of the system. It puts less focus on its shorthand system, which is not necessary. If you already have had exposure to Just Intonation, the FJS will be extremely easy for you to learn.
